The Shadowy Heist


Detective Arjun was renowned as the best in the business. His sharp mind and keen eye for detail never missed a clue. His reputation preceded him, and he was frequently called upon to solve the most challenging cases.
One day, Arjun received an urgent call from the police chief about a series of bank robberies that had been terrorizing the city. The thieves were cunning, leaving no trace behind and always managing to evade capture just in time. The chief was desperate and knew that Arjun was the only one who could crack the case.
